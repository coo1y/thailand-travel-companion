Useful Thai Phrases

**Thai Phrases:** - สวัสดี (sà-wàt-dee): Hello - ขอบคุณ (khàwp-khun): Thank you - ใช่ (chai): Yes - ไม่ใช่ (mai chai): No - ไม่เป็นไร (mai bpen rai): No problem/It's alright

Travel Tips

**Travel Tips:** - Always remove shoes before entering temples. - Insist on the meter in taxis to avoid overcharging. - Street food is delicious and cheap; opt for stalls with long lines, indicative of higher quality. - Negotiate prices respectfully at markets, as haggling is expected. - Carry a lightweight rain jacket; weather can be unpredictable.

Emergency Contact

**Emergency Contacts:** - Police: 191 - Ambulance: 1669 - Fire: 199 - Tourist Police: 1155
